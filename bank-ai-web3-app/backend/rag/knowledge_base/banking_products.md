## Savings Accounts (Latest Benchmarks from the People's Bank of China, as of January 31, 2026)
- **Demand Deposit**: Interest rate 0.05%, available for withdrawal at any time, interest accrued quarterly
- **Time Deposit (Lump-Sum Deposit and Withdrawal)**: 0.95% for 1 year, 1.25% for 3 years, 1.30% for 5 years; principal and interest withdrawn in a lump sum at maturity
- **Installment Savings Deposit**: 0.65% for 1 year, 0.88% for 3 years, 0.88% for 5 years; fixed monthly deposits, lump-sum withdrawal at maturity

## Loan Products (Based on PBOC Loan Prime Rate (LPR), as of January 31, 2026)
- **Personal Housing Loan**: Starting rate 3.05%–3.2% for first-home buyers (based on a 3.5% 5-year+ LPR plus/minus points), higher rates for second homes, maximum term 30 years; weighted average interest rate of new loans issued nationwide in Q4 2025 was 3.06%
- **Auto Loan**: Starting rate 3.8%, as low as 1.99% for qualified customers with manufacturer subsidies, maximum term 5 years
- **Personal Consumer Loan**: Starting rate 2.73% (down to around 2% for premium customers with fiscal discounts), maximum credit line RMB 1,000,000, drawdown and repayment on demand

## Investment Products (Mainstream Ranges in Chinese Market, January 2026)
- **Money Market Fund**: Annualized yield 1.8%–2.5%, low risk, strong liquidity, flexible subscription and redemption
- **Bond Fund**: Annualized yield 2.5%–4.0%, low-to-medium risk, suitable for conservative investors
- **Equity Fund**: Annualized yield 4.0%–8.0%, medium-to-high risk, large return volatility subject to market risks

## Credit Cards (Mainstream Products in Chinese Market, January 2026)
- **Standard Card**: Annual fee RMB 0, 1x reward points, no supplementary benefits, suitable for daily small-value purchases
- **Gold Card**: Annual fee RMB 100–200, 1.5x reward points, basic benefits included (e.g., roadside assistance, shopping discounts)
- **Platinum Card**: Annual fee RMB 800–1000, 2x reward points, including airport lounge access, premium hotel privileges, dedicated customer service and other perks
